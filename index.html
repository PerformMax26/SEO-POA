Phase 1 · Month 1–2
Technical SEO Foundation

Build The Foundation

You can't rank a broken house. Since the site is still in staging, this is the best time to get every technical detail right before launch.

🏗️
URL Architecture
Flat, descriptive URL hierarchy. /mushroom-coffee/, /vs/ryze/, /benefits/lions-mane/ — structured for both crawlers and humans.
⚡
Core Web Vitals
LCP under 2.5s, CLS near zero, FID under 100ms. Compress all images to WebP, lazy load below-fold assets, eliminate render-blocking resources before launch.
🏷️
Schema Markup
Product, Review, FAQ, and Article schemas from day one. Rich snippets appear in Google immediately and dramatically lift CTR vs plain blue links.
📱
Mobile-First Design
Google indexes mobile first. Every page, every CTA, every font size must be tested on mobile before launch. No pinch-to-zoom permitted.
🗺️
Sitemap & Robots.txt
XML sitemap created before indexing. Robots.txt properly configured. Submit to Google Search Console on launch day — not after traffic drops.
📊
Tracking Stack
GA4 + Google Search Console + Hotjar/MS Clarity installed pre-launch. You need baseline data from day one to measure everything that follows.
🔁
Canonical Tags
All product variant pages (flavour, size, subscription vs one-time) need canonical tags pointing to the master URL. Prevent duplicate content from hurting rankings.
🔒
HTTPS + Security
SSL certificate active, HSTS headers set, mixed content errors resolved. Google treats HTTPS as a ranking signal and browsers actively warn users on HTTP pages.

Phase 4 · Month 3 onwards
Link Building Strategy

Authority Acquisition

Modern link building is strategic and relationship-driven. Forget bulk — every link should come from a source that real potential customers actually read.

📰
Health & Wellness Editorial
Mindbodygreen, Healthline, Verywell Fit, Well+Good. These carry massive domain authority and are trusted by your exact target audience. Pitch ingredient science stories.
🧬
Biohacking Publications
Ben Greenfield Fitness, Examine.com, Huberman Lab adjacent blogs. The biohacker audience is your highest-LTV customer — meet them where they already read.
🥗
Dietitian & RD Blogs
Registered Dietitian sites are gold for E-E-A-T signals. Google weights health-adjacent links from credentialed professionals more heavily in the wellness space.
🎁
Product Gifting → Reviews
Send product to micro-influencer bloggers (5K–50K audience) in exchange for honest reviews with contextual backlinks. Scales affordably and generates authentic content.
📣
HARO / Connectively
Respond to journalist queries about coffee, adaptogens, wellness, morning routines. One Forbes or Business Insider mention can generate 10x domain authority boost.
📊
Original Research & Data
"We surveyed 500 people switching from coffee to mushroom coffee." Own data earns passive press links organically. One study can generate 20–50 backlinks if picked up correctly.
✍️
Guest Posts
Wellness, nutrition, and productivity sites. Target DR40+ domains. One well-placed guest post in a relevant niche publication beats 50 low-quality directory links.
☕
Roundup Inclusion
Get listed in "best mushroom coffee" roundup articles ranking on page 1. Outreach to these authors — offer product samples and updated info. This is where buying decisions happen.
Phase 5 · Month 2–3
On-Page & Product Page SEO

Optimise Where It Converts

Product pages are where traffic becomes revenue. They need full SEO treatment — not an afterthought.

🏷️
Keyword-Rich Product Titles
"Organic Mushroom Coffee Blend — Lion's Mane, Reishi & Cordyceps" tells Google and the customer exactly what this is and why it matters. No generic names.
✍️
Unique Product Descriptions
Never use manufacturer copy. Every product description written from scratch, targeting specific long-tail keywords and speaking directly to each buyer persona's pain points.
⭐
Review Schema
Embed real customer reviews with structured markup. Star ratings appear in Google results immediately, dramatically lifting CTR before you even rank for anything.
🔬
Ingredient Transparency
Dedicated ingredient sections on product pages target "what's in mushroom coffee" queries AND build trust. Two birds, one section. This is a gap all three competitors underinvest in.
❓
FAQ Schema on Products
Every product page needs an FAQ with schema. Capture People Also Ask placements for product-specific queries. These are prime real estate Google hands out for free.
🔗
Internal Linking Network
Every blog post links back to relevant product and category pages. Every category links to pillar content. Build a topical cluster web that flows authority to conversion pages.
Phase 6 · Ongoing from Day 1
AI Search & GEO Optimisation

Rank in AI Search

In 2026, you're not just optimising for Google — you're optimising for AI Overviews, Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, and voice. This is where your competitors have zero strategy.

🎯
Answer-First Structure
Every piece of content must deliver a clear, direct answer in the first paragraph. AI models and featured snippets both reward the same thing: answer the question immediately, then elaborate. Structure all content: question → direct answer → supporting explanation → evidence.
🤖
People Also Ask Domination
Systematically scrape PAA boxes for every seed keyword. Create dedicated FAQ sections answering each question directly. PAA appears on nearly every SERP in this category — these are free top-3 placements waiting to be claimed.
🌐
Brand Entity Building
Unlinked brand mentions across the web feed AI models like ChatGPT and Perplexity. Get listed everywhere: Healthline best-of lists, Forbes Health, Good Housekeeping, Wikipedia adjacent pages. AI citations = brand authority = more organic traffic.
🎤
Voice Search Optimisation
Nearly 50% of searches in 2026 are voice or conversational. Write content in natural language. Target "how do I make mushroom coffee" not just "mushroom coffee recipe." Conversational long-tail keywords convert better AND rank in voice results.
🏆
E-E-A-T Signal Stacking
Google's "Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness" framework decides who ranks in health/wellness. Author bios with credentials, cited studies, transparent ingredient sourcing, and third-party certifications all stack E-E-A-T signals continuously.

Launch Priorities

5 Quick Wins From Day One

Don't wait for the full strategy to kick in. These five actions generate measurable SEO results within the first 30 days of launch.

01
Publish Comparison Pages Before Launch
Get [Brand] vs Ryze, [Brand] vs MUD\WTR, and [Brand] vs Four Sigmatic live from day one. These rank within weeks and are immediately actionable for buyers already in-market.
02
FAQ Schema on Homepage & Products
Rich snippets show up in Google fast and dramatically improve CTR before you're even ranking properly. This is the easiest early-stage SEO win available.
03
Submit Sitemap to GSC on Launch Day
Don't let Google find your site by accident. Submit your sitemap to Search Console the moment the site goes live. Speeds up indexing by days or weeks.
04
10+ Verified Reviews With Schema Before Indexing
Star ratings appear immediately in search results when schema is live. Specificity matters — "2,847 reviews" outperforms "thousands of reviews" every time.
05
Google Business Profile (If Any Physical Presence)
Even for DTC brands with a registered address, a GBP adds a local SEO signal, enables Google reviews, and adds credibility to brand search queries from day one.
